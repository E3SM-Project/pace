About

PACE is a web-enabled framework that summarizes performance data collected from Energy Exascale Earth System Model (E3SM) experiments to derive insights and presents them through a portal.
PACE serves as a central hub of E3SM performance data and facilitates tracking performance benchmarks and simulation campaigns of interest.
It enables interactive exploration including deep-dive into performance of different parallel processes and threads.
PACE is designed to help identify bottlenecks and targets for performance engineering and optimization as well as drive research on load balancing and process layouts.

Architecture


Performance data from simulations executed on supported supercomputers is automatically uploaded to PACE.
PACE processes and ingests that information into the backend database. Finally, an user can interactively explore the performance data through the web portal.
